# Borne Systems — Business Credit Building Master Plan
*Created: April 5, 2026*

## The Reality
Starting from scratch on business credit. Personal credit ~550. Existing debt. Path takes 6-18 months — but there IS a clear path.

## Stage 1 — Business Identity (Week 1-2)

### 1. Get D-U-N-S Number (free, 24 hrs)
- Dun & Bradstreet — dnb.com
- This is your business's SSN for credit
- Without it, nothing reports to business credit bureaus
- Action: Go to dnb.com → search your LLC name first (it may already exist)

### 2. Open Dedicated Business Bank Account
- Separate ALL business finances from personal immediately
- Options: Bluevine, Found, Lili, Chase, BOA

### 3. Register with Business Credit Bureaus
- D&B (comes with DUNS number)
- Experian Business: experian.com/business
- Equifax Business: equifax.com/business
- These are separate from personal credit bureaus

### 4. SAM.gov Registration
- Free at sam.gov
- Required for VOSB/SDVOSB certification
- Takes 1-2 weeks to approve

## Stage 2 — Build Business Credit (Month 1-6)

### Credit Hierarchy

| Layer | What | Who | Reports to |
|-------|------|-----|------------|
| Net 30 vendors | Buy now, pay in 30 days | Supply companies, wholesalers | D&B, Experian |
| Secured business card | Deposit = limit | BofA, First National, Valley | Experian, Equifax |
| Unsecured business card | Based on business revenue | Brex, Ramp, Divvy | D&B, Experian |
| Business loan | Based on revenue + credit | SBA, banks | All bureaus |

### Month 1-2 — Starter Accounts (no credit check)
- Amazon Business account (Net 30 → builds tradeline history)
- Uline, Grainger, or supply vendor with easy approval
- First National Bank Secured Business Card — deposit $500-$2,000
- BofA Business Advantage Secured — 1% cash back

### Month 3-4 — Graduate to No-Personal-Guarantee Cards
- **Brex** — uses business revenue + growth, NOT personal credit score
- **Ramp** — business financials only, requires $75K+ revenue typically

### Month 6+ — Traditional Cards
- Chase Ink Business (need 680+ personal)
- American Express Business (need good personal credit)
- Capital One Spark

## Stage 3 — Veteran Certification (Month 1-3)

### VOSB — Veteran-Owned Small Business
- 51%+ owned by one or more veterans
- Service-connected disability NOT required
- Access to VA Vets First set-aside contracts

### SDVOSB — Service-Disabled VOSB ← pursue this
- Same + service-connected disability (even 0% rated counts)
- Broader contracting opportunities government-wide

### Process
1. SAM.gov registration (1-2 weeks) — free
2. SBA VetCert application (2-4 weeks) — veterans.certify.sba.gov
3. Get certified
4. Bid on federal contracts
5. Annual renewal

---

## Stage 4 — Loans and Financing (Month 6-18)

### SBA Microloan
- Up to $50,000
- Rate: 8-13%
- Requirement: 1 year business, good credit, business plan
- Path: Build 6 months revenue, apply

### SBA 7(a)
- Up to $5 million
- Rate: Prime + 2.75%
- Requirement: 2+ years business, strong revenue
- Path: Build 18+ months, show revenue growth

### CDVA Loan (veteran-specific)
- Through Department of Veterans Affairs
- More favorable terms for veteran-owned businesses
- Pursue once VOSB certified

### Business Line of Credit
- $10K-$100K revolving
- Most banks offer once you have business history

## Stage 5 — Personal Credit Recovery

### Priority Actions
1. Pull free reports at AnnualCreditReport.com — check for errors
2. Pay down credit card balances below 30% utilization
3. Don't close old accounts — long history helps
4. Add utility or streaming service (Experian Boost)
5. Wait 90 days after paying down debt before applying

### Personal Credit Targets
- 650 → Brex, some SBA loans
- 680 → Chase Ink, traditional business cards
- 720+ → Best rates, largest amounts

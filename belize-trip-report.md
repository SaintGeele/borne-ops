## Things to Do

### Top Activities

1. **Hol Chan Marine Reserve + Shark Ray Alley**
   - World-class snorkeling
   - Swim with rays & turtles
   - Book day before arrival

2. **Secret Beach**
   - Relaxed, less touristy
   - Good swimming
   - Access via golf cart

3. **Golf Cart Exploration**
   - Rent: ~$75/day
   - Best way to explore island

4. **Belize Chocolate Company**
   - Chocolate-making class

5. **San Pedro Town**
   - Walkable downtown
   - Shops, bars, live music

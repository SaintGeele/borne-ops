## The Problem (Why They Need This)

You already know the feeling — a homeowner calls, you send an estimate, and then... nothing. No reply. No decline. Just silence.

The truth: **85% of home service quotes go cold because no one follows up within 3 days.** Your techs did the hard part. The estimate was solid. But the customer moved on to the next contractor who followed up.

AI Receptionist handles the front end — answering calls, booking appointments, capturing leads. QuoteHook handles what happens after the estimate goes out.

## One-Pager Description

QuoteHook is an automated estimate follow-up system built for home service businesses. When you send a quote to a potential customer, QuoteHook automatically follows up via text and email at Days 1, 3, 7, and 14 — without you lifting a finger.

## Messaging Guardrails

**Do:**
- Lead with the pain: leads going cold after estimates
- Keep it practical, not hype-y
- Speak to the tradesperson, not the owner (unless the owner is the tech)
- Emphasize automation: "set it and forget it"
- Mention opt-out safety for customer trust

**Don't:**
- Use enterprise SaaS language ("synergy," "pipeline optimization")
- Overpromise close rates
- Compare directly to HubSpot, Salesforce, or generic CRMs
- Sound like a generic marketing tool — this is built for the trades

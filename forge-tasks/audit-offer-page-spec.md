## SECTION 6: FAQ

**Headline:** "Common Questions"

**Q: What's the difference between the $497 audit and the $5,000 audit?**
A: The $497 self-service audit is a structured questionnaire plus a 30-minute call — it's fast, focused, and gives you your top 3 opportunities with ROI estimates. The $5,000 full audit is a 2-week engagement with interviews, process mapping, a complete opportunity matrix, and a full roadmap. It's for businesses that want comprehensive transformation, not just a quick assessment.

**Q: Do we have to implement after the audit?**
A: No. The audit is standalone. You'll walk away with a clear picture of where your AI stands and what to do about it. If you want to implement with us, we'll send a proposal. If not, you're under no obligation.

**Q: What if the audit finds we don't have any big opportunities?**
A: That happens. And we'll tell you that honestly. Sometimes your setup is already strong — in which case, the audit confirms you're doing the right things. We'd rather tell you the truth than oversell you on problems you don't have.

**Q: How long does this take on our end?**
A: The self-service audit requires about 30 minutes of your time to complete the questionnaire, plus a 30-minute call. The full audit requires about 2 hours of your time total (interviews and review sessions) over a 2-week period.

**Q: What if we're already using AI? Can you still audit it?**
A: Especially if you're already using AI. Most of our audit clients already have AI — they just don't know if it's still working. The audit tells you exactly where you stand.

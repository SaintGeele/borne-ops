# Audit Report Template Spec
**Purpose:** Client-facing document delivered after completing the AI Discovery Audit
**Format:** Canva or Notion (recommend Canva for visual polish, Notion for interactive version)
**Length:** 10–15 pages or Notion database views

---

## Document Identity

**Title:** [Client Name] AI Opportunity Report
**Subtitle:** A structured assessment of your current AI landscape, top opportunities, and recommended roadmap
**Date:** [Audit completion date]
**Prepared by:** Borne Systems
**Confidentiality:** Prepared exclusively for [Client Name]

---

## SECTION 1: Executive Summary (1 page)

### Purpose
Give the decision-maker a 2-minute read that captures the entire audit in one place.

### Layout
- Clean, branded cover section
- Pull-quote box with the #1 finding
- 3-bullet "Top Opportunities" summary
- Quick wins vs. big swings table (mini version)
- Recommended next step

### Content
**Opening paragraph (2–3 sentences):**
"We spent two weeks reviewing [Client Name]'s current AI infrastructure, interviewing your team, and mapping the highest-impact opportunities for AI optimization. This report captures what we found — and what we recommend you do about it."

**Top 3 Opportunities (bulleted):**
1. [Opportunity 1] — [Estimated impact: $X/month or X hours/week]
2. [Opportunity 2] — [Estimated impact: $X/month or X hours/week]
3. [Opportunity 3] — [Estimated impact: $X/month or X hours/week]

**Quick Wins vs Big Swings:**
| Type | Opportunity | Estimated Timeline | Estimated Investment |
|------|-------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| Quick Win | [Opportunity] | 30 days | $[monthly cost] |
| Big Swing | [Opportunity] | 90 days | $[one-time + monthly] |

**Recommended Next Step:**
"Based on our findings, we recommend starting with [Quick Win #1]. We can begin within [X] days of agreement."

## SECTION 2: Stakeholder Interview Findings (1–2 pages)

### Purpose
Capture the leadership perspective — strategic goals, known pain points, and decision criteria.

### Content
**Interview summary:**
- Who we spoke with
- What their stated goals are for the next 12 months
- What they perceive as the biggest operational challenge
- What success looks like for them

**Key quotes (pull 2–3 verbatim quotes from interviews):**
- "[Quote about their goal]"
- "[Quote about their frustration]"

**Alignment assessment:**
- Where leadership's priorities match frontline reality
- Where there's a gap between strategy and execution

---

## SECTION 3: Employee & Process Findings (1–2 pages)

### Purpose
Capture the on-the-ground reality — what's actually happening vs. what leadership thinks is happening.

### Content
**Process map summary:**
- How new leads enter the system (phone, web, referral)
- Where and how leads are handled today
- Where the handoff gaps are
- Where time is being wasted on manual tasks

**Staff pain points (anonymized):**
- What team members identified as their biggest friction points
- Where they see leads falling through the cracks
- What they'd fix if they had the tools

**Gap analysis:**
- What leadership thinks is happening vs. what's actually happening
- Where the process breaks down between customer contact and conversion

---

## SECTION 4: AI Opportunity Matrix (2–3 pages)

### Purpose
The core analytical output. Prioritize all identified opportunities by impact vs. effort.

### Layout
**Visual:** 2×2 quadrant matrix
- X-axis: Effort to Implement (Low → High)
- Y-axis: Revenue Impact (Low → High)

**Quadrants:**
- **Quick Wins (High Impact / Low Effort)** — Do immediately
- **Big Swings (High Impact / High Effort)** — Plan for 90–180 days
- **Fill-Ins (Low Impact / Low Effort)** — Do if capacity allows
- **Reconsider (Low Impact / High Effort)** — Skip or deprioritize

### Full opportunity table
| # | Opportunity | Category | Impact | Effort | Timeline | Est. Investment | Est. ROI |
|---|------------|----------|--------|--------|---------|-----------------|---------|
| 1 | AI call handling upgrade | Acquisition | $X/mo | Low | 30 days | $297/mo | 3–6 months |
| 2 | Automated lead follow-up | Nurture | $X/mo | Low | 14 days | $149/mo | 1–3 months |
| 3 | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |

### For each opportunity include:
- What the problem is (in their words)
- What the solution does
- How it maps to their specific pain
- What the estimated revenue impact is
- What the investment required is
- What the payback period is

## Design Notes

**Canva version:**
- Use Borne Systems brand colors
- Clean, professional — not flashy
- Pull-quote boxes for key findings
- Tables with alternating row colors for readability
- Section dividers between each major section
- 1–2 charts/visuals (opportunity matrix, ROI comparison)

**Notion version:**
- Database view of opportunities (filterable by category, impact, timeline)
- Inline page for each opportunity with full detail
- Roadmap view (timeline format)
- Linked ROI calculator spreadsheet
- Status tracking for implementation progress post-sale

**Both versions:**
- PDF export capability
- Editable for customization per client
- Branded header/footer on every page

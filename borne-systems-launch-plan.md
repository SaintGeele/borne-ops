## Marketing Tactics by Phase

### Soft Launch Tactics
1. **Direct outreach** — Email 10 contacts, ask for referrals
2. **Case study** — Publish Carson Aesthetics results
3. **LinkedIn organic** — 3 posts about AI receptionist demo

### Public Launch Tactics
1. **Google Ads** — $200 test, $50/mo maintenance
2. **LinkedIn Company Page** — Post 3x weekly, engage in comments
3. **Local Facebook groups** — 5 posts in local business groups
4. **Cold email** — 20 prospects, personalized, value-first
5. **Referral program** — Ask every client for 1 referral

### Post-Launch Tactics
1. **Nurture emails** — 5-email sequence for prospects
2. **Content marketing** — Blog posts on AI + security for SMBs
3. **Industry events** — 1 meetup/month (optional)
4. **Partner referrals** — Web designers, accountants who refer clients

## Personalization Cheat Sheet

**Always customize:**
- [First Name] - Prospect's first name
- [Business Name] - Their business name
- [City] - Their location
- [business type] - Their industry (dentist, med spa, etc.)
- [similar business] - A relevant case study or similar business

**Tone reminders:**
- Write like you're texting a colleague, not pitching a corporation
- Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences max)
- Lead with THEIR problem, not your service
- One clear CTA per message
- Confidence without arrogance

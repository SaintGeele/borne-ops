## Recommended New Products (Ranked)

### Tier 1 — Quick Wins / High Value

**1. QuoteHook** *(already planned)*
Automates quote follow-up sequences. SMS + email at Day 1/3/7/14 intervals.
Linked to AI Receptionist. $149-249/mo add-on.
Status: Planned, awaiting TCPA legal review.

**2. Gauge — Operations Monitor**
Monitors business metrics, tracks goal progress, sends daily standup summaries.
Uses Notion as backend. Simple build — state tracking + daily digest.
Free or $29/mo.

**3. ReviewFlow**
Automated review request engine. Triggers SMS/email after confirmed customer appointment asking for Google/Yelp review. No对抗 — just systematic review generation.
$49/mo add-on or standalone.

### Tier 2 — Medium Effort / Strong ICP Fit

**4. IntakePro**
Multi-channel lead capture form widget. Web + SMS. Routes leads to Notion CRM automatically. Includes basic lead scoring.
$99/mo standalone, $49/mo add-on to AI Receptionist.

**5. Renegade — Penetration Testing Persona**
Red team operator persona prompt. OSCP-aligned methodology. Structured pen test workflow: recon → enum → exploit → doc → report.
Can be sold as an educational tool or red team co-pilot.
$149 one-time or $49/mo.

**6. Scout — Competitor Intelligence Agent**
Monitors competitor websites, pricing pages, job postings, and news. Weekly digest. Uses Tavily/research tools.
$79/mo.

### Tier 3 — Larger Builds / Platform Plays

**7. Iris — Customer Success Agent**
Onboarding automation. New client welcome sequence, day-1/3/7 check-ins, usage alerts, renewal reminders.
Fills the gap between Sales Agent and Support Agent.
$99/mo.

**8. LocalRank Tracker**
Tracks Google Business Profile rankings, citation consistency, local keyword positions.
Integrates with BrightLocal API or builds its own citation tracker.
$79/mo.

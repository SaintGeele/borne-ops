## DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT

### Opening
"Thanks for making time. I'm not here to pitch you anything — I just want to understand how your business operates. Can you walk me through a typical day?"

### Key Questions
- "What tasks take the most time that don't directly make you money?"
- "How many calls do you miss in a week? What's your average job value?"
- "What happens when a lead calls after hours?"
- "Do you have any AI tools right now? How are they working?"
- "If you could fix one thing in your operation tomorrow, what would it be?"

### Objection Handlers
- "I don't have time" → "That's exactly what we're trying to fix. 15 minutes now could save you hours later."
- "Too expensive" → "What would it be worth to not miss another lead? Let's figure out if there's a problem worth solving first."
- "I already have AI" → "Great — most businesses do. The question is whether it's actually working. Want to find out?"

### Close
"If what you tell me today suggests we can actually help, I'd recommend starting with our $497 audit — it gives you a complete picture of where AI is costing you money and exactly how to fix it. No obligation either way. Sound fair?"

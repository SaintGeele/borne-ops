## BOTTOM LINE

Borne Systems currently operates as an **AI automation agency** (selling tools). The course reveals we need to shift to **AI consultancy model** — adding a diagnostic/audit layer in front and a maintenance layer underneath. This is how we go from selling $99/mo tools to landing $5K-$50K audits + $3K+/mo maintenance retainers.

## THE OPPORTUNITY

### What's Missing (and Why It Matters)
1. **No Audit/Diagnosis product** — we can't enter conversations with prospects without "selling something"
2. **No Maintenance layer** — we install and leave, no recurring relationship
3. **No Discovery process** — we pitch products, not diagnose problems
4. **No Positioning as consultants** — we're a vendor, not a strategic partner

### What This Costs Us
- Can't close larger clients (need audit credibility)
- Can't justify premium pricing (audit = proof of value)
- No recurring revenue (maintenance = $3K/client/month)
- Competitors who DO offer audits win by default

### What This Enables
- $5K-$50K audit contracts (entry point)
- $5K-$500K implementation deals (after audit)
- $3K/client/month maintenance (recurring revenue)
- 80%+ profit margins (consultancy model)
- Competitive moat (nobody in our ICP is doing this)

## NEW PRODUCT PACKAGE — BORN-4 STAGES

### Stage 1: AI Discovery Audit — $5,000-$15,000
**What:** 2-week structured assessment of the client's business
**Deliverables:**
- Stakeholder interview summary (leadership perspective)
- Employee interview summary (on-the-ground reality)
- Process map (acquisition, delivery, support)
- AI Opportunity Matrix (quick wins vs big swings)
- Recommended roadmap (30/90/180 day plan)
- ROI projections for each recommendation

**Process:**
- Week 1: Conduct 5-15 interviews (30-45 min each, recorded)
- Week 2: Map opportunities, build matrix, validate with client, present roadmap

**Pricing:**
- 1-10 employees: $5,000
- 11-50 employees: $10,000
- 51-200 employees: $15,000+
- Enterprise: $25,000-$50,000+

**This converts to:** Implementation contract OR standalone advisory relationship

## SALES PROCESS REDESIGN — CHASE

### Current Process (Broken)
```
Cold outreach → Demo → Close → Done
```

### New Process (Based on Vibe Audit)
```
Cold outreach → Discovery call → Audit proposal → Audit ($$$) →
Implementation proposal → Implement → Maintenance ($$$/mo)
```

### Discovery Call Script (30 min)
**Goal:** Qualify + schedule audit, NOT close

**Agenda:**
1. 5 min: Understand their business (NOT pitch)
2. 10 min: "Walk me through your current customer acquisition flow"
3. 10 min: "Where do you lose the most leads / deals / revenue?"
4. 5 min: "If I could show you exactly where AI would have the biggest impact on your business, with math — would you be open to that?"

**Qualifying question:** "On a scale of 1-10, how frustrated are you with how your business handles [their specific pain]?"

**Close:** "It sounds like you've got some clear opportunities. We'd need to do a proper audit to give you specific recommendations — we charge $5K for that. Would you want to move forward with that, or should we stay in touch?"

# Lead Magnet: Free AI Opportunity Quiz

**Concept:** "How Many AI Failures Is Your Business One Update Away From?"
**Alternative title:** "The AI Maintenance Readiness Quiz"

---

## Concept Overview

A 5-question quiz that diagnoses where a small service business stands with their current AI — and identifies the silent gaps most business owners don't know they have.

**Core value proposition:** "In 5 minutes, you'll know exactly where AI is costing you money — and whether your current setup is one update away from failure."

**Lead capture:** Name + Email + Business Type + Current AI tools (if any) → Instant score + email sequence

---

## Quiz Questions

### Q1: How long have you been using AI in your business?
- Less than 6 months
- 6–12 months
- 1–2 years
- More than 2 years
- We're not currently using AI

**What this tells us:** Longer usage = higher probability of drift. Also tells us their current stage (evaluating vs. already implemented).

---

### Q2: Do you know your current AI's call handling rate or automation accuracy?
- Yes, I track it monthly
- I check it occasionally
- I have no way to measure it
- We don't use AI for this

**What this tells us:** If they don't measure it, it's probably drifting. This is the core failure mode.

---

### Q3: Has your team noticed any of the following in the last 3 months? (Select all that apply)
- More missed calls than before
- AI giving outdated information
- Customers complaining about automated responses
- Leads falling through the cracks
- Response times getting slower
- None of these

**What this tells us:** Symptom identification. If they select multiple, they're already in drift mode.

---

### Q4: When was the last time someone reviewed and updated your AI configuration?
- Within the last month
- 1–3 months ago
- 3–6 months ago
- I don't think it's ever been updated
- I'm not sure who handles this

**What this tells us:** Maintenance accountability gap. If they don't know who owns it, nobody does.

---

### Q5: What would it be worth to you (per month) to know your AI was consistently performing at peak capacity?
- $500/month
- $1,000/month
- $2,500/month
- $5,000/month
- I don't know

**What this tells us:** Budget qualification and stakes-setting for the value conversation.

---

## Scoring

**0–2 "Good" answers:** Your AI is either new or actively maintained. But drift starts fast — you need a baseline assessment. (Score: "Optimized" — lead toward Audit Readiness Checklist)

**3–4 "At Risk" answers:** You likely have drift happening right now, even if you haven't noticed. Time for a proper audit. (Score: "At Risk" — lead toward Free Audit + discovery call)

**4–5 "Failing" answers:** Your AI is likely costing you real revenue every month. You need an audit immediately. (Score: "Critical" — lead toward paid $497 self-service audit ASAP)

## Alternative Lead Magnet: AI Audit Readiness Checklist

**What it is:** A downloadable PDF showing the 15-point checklist we use in our full AI audits — things like call handling rates, lead capture metrics, configuration review cadence, etc.

**Use case:** Better for evaluators who aren't ready for a quiz — they want to self-assess on their own time. Less engagement but higher lead quality.

**Gating:** Email capture to download. Same nurture sequence as quiz, but starts with "here's your checklist" instead of "here are your results."

**Recommended:** Run both simultaneously. Quiz = volume play. Checklist = high-intent play. Test which converts better to discovery calls.

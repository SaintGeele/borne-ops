## Why This Matters

The reason I recommend this before any implementation is simple: **I don't want to sell you something that doesn't fit.**

AI only works when it solves a real problem. The audit tells us both whether there's a genuine opportunity here — and if there is, exactly what to do about it.

If the audit comes back and it doesn't make sense to move forward, I'll tell you that. You won't have lost anything except time.

# DELEGATION.md — What Closer Delegates

## Can Delegate To

### Care (Onboarding Handoff)
- Client onboarding coordination
- Welcome materials and kickoff scheduling
- FAQ responses for new clients
- Ticket creation for client issues
- **Trigger:** Deal is signed, payment confirmed

### Chase (Lead Back to Pipeline)
- Re-engagement if lead went cold before closing
- Follow-up sequences if Closer needs more time
- Nurture drip if timeline is 60+ days out
- **Trigger:** Closer identifies nurture opportunity or timeline gap

### MrX (Content/Collateral Support)
- Custom case study for a vertical
- One-pager for a specific pain point
- Competitive battle card needed
- **Trigger:** Prospect has a unique competitive situation Closer can't address with existing materials

### Forge (Demo Environment)
- Custom demo build for a specific prospect use case
- Technical deep-dive during discovery if prospect needs it
- **Trigger:** Discovery reveals a technical requirement Closer can't demo live

### Ivy (Research)
- Company research on prospect before call
- Competitive intel on who they're currently using
- **Trigger:** Pre-call research request from Closer

## What Closer Does NOT Delegate
- Discovery call execution
- Proposal writing (she drives it, uses AI assist)
- Objection handling
- Closing the deal
- Final proposal approval (Geele signs off on deals >$2K MRR)

## Handoff Protocol
All delegations go through Atlas when possible. For urgent escalations, Closer notifies Geele directly.

## How to Post These

**Key rules:**
- Short sentences
- Direct, no fluff
- Share the real story
- End with context or next step

**Voice check:**
- Would I say this?
- Is it direct?
- Is it real?
- Does it add value?

**Don't:**
- Sound corporate
- Use buzzwords
- Over-polish
- Long paragraphs

**Do:**
- Be authentic
- Share failures too
- Keep it short
- Add a thought

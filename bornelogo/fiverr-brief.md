# Fiverr Logo Design Brief — Borne Systems

## Company
**Name:** Borne Systems
**Website:** bornesystems.com
**Tagline:** "AI that works the way your business does." / "YOUR BUSINESS RUNS ON → AUTOPILOT"

## What We Do
AI automation tools for small service businesses (1-50 employees). Primary product: AI Receptionist (24/7 voice automation). Also: lead automation, security assessments, workflow automation.

## Industry
AI automation / B2B SaaS / Technology

## Target Audience
Small service businesses — HVAC, plumbing, roofing, landscaping, contractors. CT, NY, NJ, MA.

## Founder
Geele Evans — U.S. Military veteran, systems engineer, OSCP candidate.

## Brand Personality
Practical. Strong. No fluff. Engineered, not marketed. Veteran-owned. Security-first. Calm confidence.

## Brand Colors (MUST USE)
- **Primary:** Electric Blue `#2563eb`
- **Background:** Near Black `#030406`
- **Accent:** Light Blue `#60a5fa`
- **Text:** White `#ffffff` / Muted `#8892b0`
- Website: www.bornesystems.com — use as style reference

## Brand Fonts
- **Headlines:** Funnel Display (bold, geometric, modern)
- **Accents:** Silkscreen (pixelated, tech)

## Style Direction
Clean, flat, geometric. Think: Linear, Stripe, Vercel. NOT gradients, NOT 3D, NOT shadows. Professional B2B tech. Dark-mode dominant.

## What We Like
- Wordmark-style logos (company name in a distinctive, clean font)
- Icon element that encodes: automation, direction, signal, or system
- Strong tie to "autopilot" or "automated" without being literal
- Icons that work as standalone favicon/app icon
- Logos that look premium and timeless, NOT trendy

## What We DON'T Want
- Generic AI brain icons
- Glowing/neon effects
- Colorful gradients (our brand is dark + blue)
- Clipart or clip-art style elements
- Cartoons or playful elements
- Handwritten or script fonts

## Deliverables Required
1. Primary logo (full wordmark + icon)
2. Icon-only version (for profile pictures, favicon, app icons)
3. Dark background version
4. Light background version
5. All files: SVG, PNG (transparent background), and any vector format
6. Color codes used

## Concepts We Like (For Reference)
- IBM, FedEx, Linear, Stripe, Vercel — wordmark-first, geometric icon
- A bold geometric letter "B" that doubles as an arrow or signal
- A clean wordmark with Funnel Display or similar bold sans-serif

## Budget
$50-150 for quality work. Will tip for exceptional output.

# AI Receptionist — ClawMart Listing

## What It Is

A voice AI receptionist that answers your business phone 24/7, collects caller information, books appointments, handles FAQs, and sends SMS confirmations — without you lifting a finger.

Built on VAPI for voice AI and Resend for SMS. Notion-powered admin dashboard. No missed calls. No voicemail slips. No manual follow-up.

## What You Get

### Voice AI Answering
- Answers every call, day or night
- Natural conversation — caller doesn't know it's AI
- Handles common questions: hours, pricing, services, location
- Transfers to human for complex requests
- Never puts callers on hold

### Appointment Booking
- Checks calendar availability in real time
- Books, confirms, and reschedules appointments
- Sends SMS confirmation immediately after booking
- Reminds callers of upcoming appointments
- Collects: name, phone, reason for visit, preferred time

### FAQ Handling
- Pre-loaded with your business FAQ
- Answers common questions automatically
- Routes specific questions to the right person
- Learns from missed questions (you approve new responses)

### SMS Automation
- Appointment confirmation via SMS (Resend)
- Reminder texts before appointments
- Custom SMS templates per scenario
- Opt-out handling included

### Admin Dashboard (Notion)
- View all calls: answered, missed, duration, outcome
- See booked appointments in real time
- Review caller info and reason for contact
- Update FAQ and business info without code
- Basic call transcripts (optional recording)

## How It Works

```
Incoming Call
    ↓
VAPI Voice AI answers (custom greeting)
    ↓
Caller explains reason → AI collects info
    ↓
Calendar check → slot available?
    ↓
[Yes] → Book appointment → SMS confirmation via Resend
[No]  → Offer waitlist or callback
[Complex] → Transfer to human with full context
    ↓
Call logged to Notion dashboard
```

## Setup Requirements

1. **VAPI account** — voice AI provider (vapi.ai)
2. **Resend account** — SMS delivery (resend.com)
3. **Notion workspace** — admin dashboard
4. **Calendar** — Google Calendar or Cal.com for availability
5. **Phone number** — port existing or get new from VAPI

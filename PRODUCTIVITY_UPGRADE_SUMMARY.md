## 🚀 New Automation Suite

### 1. Smart Morning Prep (6:55 AM)
**What it does:**
- Checks Canvas for new assignments/deadlines
- Reviews email for professor responses  
- Identifies "must do today" priorities
- Preps personalized intel for morning brief

**Result:** Morning brief is tailored to YOUR actual situation, not generic info.

**Schedule:** Daily at 6:55 AM (5 min before main brief)

---

### 2. Job Search Pipeline (9 AM & 6 PM)
**What it does:**
- Scans for new job postings (integrates with your searches)
- Scores matches against your skills profile
- Queues high-match jobs for your review
- Tracks application status

**Result:** You review pre-qualified jobs instead of searching from scratch.

**Schedule:** Twice daily at 9:00 AM and 6:00 PM

---

### 3. Academic Deadline Guard (Hourly, 7 AM - 11 PM)
**What it does:**
- Checks all tracked assignments hourly
- Alerts for overdue items (CRITICAL)
- Alerts for items due today (URGENT)
- Alerts for items due tomorrow (WARNING)
- Tracks submission status

**Result:** Nothing falls through cracks. You get timely reminders.

**Schedule:** Every hour from 7 AM to 11 PM

**Alert Levels:**
- **CRITICAL:** Overdue items (every alert)
- **URGENT:** Due today (every alert)
- **WARNING:** Due tomorrow (daily alert)
- **NOTICE:** Due in 2 days (daily alert)

---

### 4. Lead Nurturing (Every 6 Hours)
**What it does:**
- Pulls leads from Notion database
- Updates pipeline status
- Identifies new HOT leads
- Suggests follow-up actions
- Tracks contacted vs. not contacted

**Result:** Your 50 leads are actively managed without manual tracking.

**Schedule:** 6:00 AM, 12:00 PM, 6:00 PM daily

---

### 5. Context Pre-Loader (On-Demand)
**What it does:**
- Loads current priorities before our chats
- Preps active task list
- Reviews recent activity
- Loads quick stats

**Result:** Faster, more focused conversations. I already know what's happening.

**Trigger:** When you message me

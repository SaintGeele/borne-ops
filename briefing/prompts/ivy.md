# Ivy - Research Opportunities Brief

**Task:** Provide a concise research/opportunities summary for the CEO briefing.

**Output Requirements:**
- Maximum 3 sentences
- Focus on: new leads, market opportunities, competitor moves, or research in progress
- If nothing new, state "No new opportunities identified"
- Highlight actionable items with 📌

**Context:**
- Check any active lead lists
- Note market research in progress
- Report on outreach response rates

**Deliver:** One paragraph suitable for the briefing.
